What is a Pig Butchering Scam?

The term "pig butchering" comes from how scammers "fatten up" victims with fake trust and romance, then "slaughter" them by convincing them to invest large sums in fake crypto projects. These criminals spend weeks or months building emotional connections to lower the victim's guard.

Common Crypto Scams to Watch Out For

  • Fake Crypto Wallets: Scammers often promote fake wallets designed to steal private keys or crypto assets.
  • Romance Scams: Pretending to be a loving partner, scammers manipulate victims into sending money or crypto investments.
  • Fake Investment Platforms: Fraudulent websites promise high returns but only serve to steal your funds.

How to Avoid Falling Victim

Protect yourself by verifying wallet addresses, using trusted platforms only, and being cautious about online relationships that rush toward investment talks. Always double-check investment opportunities and never share your private keys.

Remember: if it sounds too good to be true, it probably is.
